Android Customization

Android Homescreens

The homescreen on android is highly customizable,you can add widgets,place app icons anywhere on the app grid,change icon packs,use transistions,change the grid size,and more.

Icon packs

Icon packs replace your default app icons.App icons give your icons a different look.

Launchers are homescreen replacements. Third party launchers can allow you to cutomize your homescreen more then your default launcher would,and give you additional features.Some examples of feature that launchers offer are universal search bars,the ability to hide apps,use gestures,change font color,etc.
